DirectRent Tanzania

An interactive MVP research dashboard for a tenant-landlord rental platform that reduces reliance on brokers, improves trust, and creates faster direct matches.

Executive Summary

  • The product connects tenants and landlords directly in one mobile-first platform.
  • The MVP focuses on listings, photos, search, trust indicators, and direct chat.
  • Revenue model: TZS 1,000 for tenants and TZS 5,000 for landlords.
  • Main launch challenge: trust, listing quality, and early supply.
  • Main advantage: faster discovery, clearer communication, and lower friction than broker-heavy search.

Problem Areas

Core Problem

Tenants often rely on scattered WhatsApp groups, referrals, and brokers. Landlords struggle to attract serious leads efficiently.

The platform creates structure, transparency, and direct communication.

Must-have MVP Features

  • Tenant and landlord onboarding
  • Property listings
  • Photo uploads
  • Search and filters
  • Listing detail page
  • Direct in-app chat
  • Basic verification badge
  • Simple subscriptions
